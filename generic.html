Neurogenetics Neurogenetics lies at the intersection of Neuroscience and Genetics, where genetic approaches are applied to the study of nervous system development, function, and plasticity. These two fields have rapidly advanced over the past few decades, especially as leaps in novel technologies have paved the way for an expansion in experimental tools.
